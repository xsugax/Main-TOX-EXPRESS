Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common logistics queries

How do I track my shipment?

Use your tracking ID (format: TOX-YYYY-XXXXXX) in our Satellite Tracking section. You'll see real-time location on a live map, full timeline history, and current ETA. Tracking updates are available 24/7.

What countries do you ship to?

TOX Express operates across 190+ countries on 6 continents. Our network of ocean freight, air cargo, and ground transport covers virtually every destination worldwide, including remote and landlocked regions.

How long does international shipping take?

Air Cargo: 1-5 days. Ocean Freight: 15-45 days. Express: same-day to 3 days. Ground Transport: varies by region. Use our Cost Calculator for exact estimates including customs clearance time.

Do you handle customs clearance?

Yes — we provide full customs brokerage services. Our licensed agents handle all documentation, duties, tariffs, and regulatory compliance so your cargo clears smoothly without delays.

Is my cargo insured during transit?

We offer multiple insurance tiers: Basic (up to $10,000), Standard (up to $50,000), and Premium (full declared value). Coverage includes damage, loss, theft, and natural disaster during transit.

Can you ship hazardous or oversized goods?

Yes. TOX Express is IATA DGR certified for hazardous materials and equipped for oversized/project cargo. We handle chemicals, lithium batteries, heavy machinery, and more — with full safety documentation.

How do I get a shipping quote?

Use our instant Cost Calculator on this page for immediate estimates. For custom quotes on large volumes or special cargo, contact our team via the contact form or email us directly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank wire transfers, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and corporate accounts with NET-30/60 terms for verified enterprise clients.
